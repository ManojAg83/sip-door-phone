Privacy Policy

Application: OKAS SIP Door Phone

1. Introduction

OKAS SIP Door Phone is a SIP-based door phone and communication application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, transmit, and protect information when you use our application.

By installing or using the application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 SIP Account Information

  • SIP username
  • SIP password
  • SIP server domain or IP address
  • Display name

This information is used solely to enable SIP-based voice and video communication.

SIP credentials are stored locally on the device and are not sold or shared for advertising.

2.2 Audio Data (Microphone Permission – RECORD_AUDIO)

Microphone access is required for two-way voice communication during active SIP sessions. Audio data is transmitted only during active sessions and routed through the configured SIP server.

2.3 Video Data (Camera Permission – CAMERA)

Camera access enables SIP video calls and live video streams. Video is transmitted only during active sessions and is not stored unless configured by the user.

2.4 Network and Connectivity Information

We may process IP address, Wi-Fi state, and connectivity status to maintain SIP registration and communication stability.

2.5 Notifications

Notifications are used to inform users of incoming calls and call status updates.

2.6 Call Management

Android call management features are used to handle SIP calls. The app does not access cellular carrier call data.

2.7 Background Services

Foreground and background services are used to maintain SIP connectivity and enable real-time communication.

2.8 Device Wake & Boot Services

The app may prevent device sleep during calls and restart SIP registration after reboot.

3. Third-Party SIP Server Processing

Communication is routed through the configured SIP server, which may be operated by your organization or a third-party provider. We do not control third-party server privacy practices.

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • SIP credentials stored locally
  • No centralized storage of voice or video
  • No centralized call recording

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade personal data. Information is transmitted only to the configured SIP server for communication.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards including secure credential storage and encrypted SIP communication (if supported).

7. Legal Basis for Processing

We process data to provide communication services requested by the user.

8. Children’s Privacy

This application is not directed to children under 13 years of age.

9. Third-Party Libraries & Services

The app may use Android system services, SIP libraries (e.g., Linphone), and push notification services.

10. International Data Transfers

Communication data may be transmitted internationally if the SIP server is located outside your country.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
